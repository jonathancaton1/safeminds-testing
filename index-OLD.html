YuLife App

Safenames has teamed up with YuLife to provide Our Employee Assistance Programme (YuMatter).

YuMatter is a confidential employee assistance programme designed to support your mental, financial, and professional wellbeing. The services provides a mental and emotional support service, with 24/7 live answer by a clinician, as well as short-term counselling and life coaching. This service is powered by Workplace Options.
