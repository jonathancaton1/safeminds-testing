Pensions

To help people save more for their retirement, all employers are now required by law to provide a workplace pension scheme for certain staff and pay money into it. Our workplace pension with Royal London is set up to help you save for your retirement.

You’ll be automatically enrolled in our pension scheme after 3 months if you:

  • earn more than £10,000 per year before tax (this is based on your contracted hours and doesn’t include any overtime)
  • are aged 22 or above (if you turn 22 when working for Safenames, you’ll be automatically enrolled in the scheme)
  • are under the State Pension Age

If you wish, you can elect not to participate in the pension scheme made available to you. If this is the case, please contact Human Resources

The minimum contributions that you must pay into your workplace pension scheme are shown in the table below – they’re currently a total contribution of 8% with at least 3% employer contribution.

Date Employer min contribution Total min contribution
From 6 April 2019 3% 8% (including 5% staff contribution)

Our current contribution rate is 4% of salary for employees. Safenames currently contributes at the rate of 4% of your salary.

Have Dinner on Us!

As a company perk that was born in 'lockdown', we provide a monthly allowance for all employees to spend at 'Just Eat'. On the 1st of each month, £20 will be automatically credited to your 'Just Eat' account. You can use the allowance to order food online for any occasion. Family dinner, daily lunch, virtual events or coffee?
We've got you covered!

Note: It’s a one time use allowance which will expire at the end of the month.
The allowance will completely refresh each month (1st) - so be sure to spend it all!

How can I order online using my Just Eat allowance?

1. Once you have been sent an invitation link and activated your account, log in to the 'Just Eat' website or app with your work email address.

2. Select pickup or delivery, find your favourite bites and continue to the checkout page by clicking "Order".

3. On the checkout page, your allowance will be automatically deducted from the total amount. If your allowance covers the full amount, the order can be completed. If the allowance doesn't cover the total amount, the remaining balance can be paid with another online payment method.

Recognition Awards

Celebrating our dedicated employees

Safenames recognises that the key factor in the success of the organisation, is the loyalty and dedication of the people who work within it. In recognition of this, Safenames is committed to celebrate with those members of staff who have reached certain service ‘milestones’ by providing a system of awards for loyal service.

Entitlement

We offer rewards in the following ways:

Additional Holiday:

Employees who have completed one year of service will receive an additional day of paid holiday for each subsequent year, up to a maximum of five days.

Gift Vouchers:

Employees are eligible to receive gift vouchers on reaching a certain number of years of service. The following list will give you an idea of the rewards based on years of completed service:

20 Years
A personalised silver cog and a Rolex watch

*The award is not mandatory – an employee may opt out of receiving the award if they wish. HR will arrange the gift voucher and presentation along with the employee’s Line Manager.

Eligibility

All office employees will be entitled to an award providing they have worked continuously without breaks for Safenames when reaching the milestones outlined above. See exceptions below. The Procedure

A member of HR will liaise with the Line Manager of those reaching 5, 10, 15 and 20 years’ service and notify them in advance of staff eligible to receive an award in that month. The Line Manager will be requested to provide HR with the personal wording to be included in the respective employee’s recognition email signed off by Adan, CEO. (In cases where the Line Manager has not provided the wording to HR by the agreed deadline, this may cause a significant delay in providing the award to the employee.) HR will liaise directly with the employee to arrange their selected award.

Upon reaching the respective milestone, the employee will receive a personalised “cog” and be featured on the office 'Family Tree'. The employee will also receive their gift voucher, on or near the date of their anniversary.

Exceptions

In circumstances where an employee has current disciplinary sanctions against them or is under warning of dismissal, it could be contradictory to reward recognition of service. Under such circumstances, the Line Manager should consult with HR for a decision to defer or withhold the award until such matters are resolved.

Legal Implications

This policy complies with The Employment Equality (Age) Regulations 2006 as the award is designed to encourage and reward loyalty through the presentation of a staff benefit. The policy has been instigated following feedback from staff review groups, employee surveys and our company culture report.
