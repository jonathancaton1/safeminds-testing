Mental Well-being

Health Assured

Health Assured is Safenames’ Employee Assistance Programme (EAP).

How does it work?

Your employee assistance programme gives you – and your family – 24/7 confidential wellbeing support, and information on a number of life-related issues.

What's included?

  • 24/7/365 unlimited access to a telephone helpline for in-the-moment access to qualified therapists.
  • Legal information: support on a range of issues including motoring, consumer, family including dependant care, wills and commercial.
  • Medical information: Nurses available to provide medical information on a range of health issues.
  • Financial and debt information: Access to experts who can provide information on a range of issues including debt, investments and pensions.

How to access the EAP

You can access this through Wisdom, an app provided by Health Assured as we as by calling them directly.
